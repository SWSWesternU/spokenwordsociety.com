Interested in Learning More?

You're in the right place.

Spoken Word Society is a community of Spoken Word Artists, Enthusiasts and Poets. Our goal is two-fold: to introduce newcomers to the fascinating world of Spoken Word and to provide a forum for more experienced Spoken Word Artists. However, perhaps more importantly, we want to foster a community where students feel SAFE to express their stories and opinions without any fear of judgement.

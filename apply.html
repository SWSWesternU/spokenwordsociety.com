Greetings.

If you are on this page, we assume you are interested in applying for our Executive Team. As a second year club, there are definitely a LOT of positions open for the taking. And we are definitely excited to have you on our team.

A few important things to keep in mind, all Executive Members will be expected to attend biweekly Executive Meetings. Attendance to the biweekly Workshops is optional (with the exception to Workshop Coordinators), however, highly recommended.

The positions of Graphics and Web Designer are the only ones requiring technical skills. In addition, only Workshop Coordinators are required to have prior experience in Spoken Word. Other than that, no prior experience or knowledge is required for the other positions.

We encourage you to apply for as many positions as interests you. You will also have an opportunity to rank them.

Last, but not least, candidates need to officially become a member if they wish to be considered for the positions. Please register through the USC Portal to join SWS before applying. If you apply before Club's Week (when registrations open), you will be sent a reminder on Monday to register for the club. Also note that the form will ask you to log into your email account.

Below, we've listed the open positions with a short description of duties.

GRAPHICS Designer(s): design flyers and other related promotional materials.

WEB Designer(s): maintain this website; upload new contributions; create pages; develop the website.

OUTREACH Coordinator: collect submissions for the club website, organize submission uploads and reach out to other clubs.

WORKSHOP Coordinator(s): plan, organize and lead writing workshops.
